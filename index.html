About

Dr. Paa Kwasi Adusei is an accomplished Engineer and Engineering Manager with a robust academic foundation and over seven years of experience in the semiconductor industry.

He holds a Bachelor's degree in Engineering Physics, a Master's in Electrical Engineering, and a Ph.D. in Materials Science and Engineering. His multidisciplinary expertise bridges the gap between advanced materials research and practical engineering solutions, enabling him to lead high-impact projects in semiconductor process development, device reliability, and innovation strategy.

As a manager, he is passionate about mentoring engineers, driving cross-functional collaboration, and delivering cutting-edge technologies that shape the future of electronics. His passion for science and research drives his ethos around engineering problem solving and project execution.
